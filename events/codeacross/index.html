Join us for Code Across 2016

Join OpenSMC, San Mateo County's Code for America Brigade, for our first CodeAcross event!

CodeAcross 2016 unites civic enthusiasts, residents, technologists, and engaged individuals around the world to solve community challenges and improve government services.

When: Join OpenSMC's CodeAcross from 12:30PM-4:30PM on Sunday, March 6, 2016.

Who Should Attend: People living/working in San Mateo County, neighbors, UX/UI designers, software engineers, local government staff, YOU!

Agenda

Find out how CivicMakers works to engage conversation and inspire ideas in the civic tech space.

Introduce OpenSMC, its mission, and weekly hack night.

Hack on OpenSMC projects.

Spend the afternoon with us. Help solve challenges. Bring new ideas.

See you there!
